I am a postdoctoral fellow, working with Dr. Albert Sunny at IIT Palakkad Technology IHub Foundation (IPTIF), Palakkad, Kerala, India. My research interests are in theoretical computer science, especially in graph theory. In particular, I am interested in Structural Graph Theory, Graph Colorings, Graph Algorithms, and Complexity Analysis. I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Information Technology Design & Manufacturing (IIITDM Chennai), Kancheepuram, India in 2017. My supervisor is Dr K Premkumar.
